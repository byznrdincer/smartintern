About smartIntern

Where skills speak louder than grades.

Who We Are

Our Mission

Bridge the gap between talented students and forward-thinking companies through skill-based hiring.

Our Vision

Equal opportunities for every student to showcase their abilities and land their dream internships.

Our Impact

Helping companies find real talent based on actual coding skills and project experience.

Why We Started

Too many skilled students were overlooked because of low GPAs or unknown universities, while companies struggled to find capable, practical developers.

smartIntern was created to flip the hiring process. We focus on **what you can build**, not just what school you attended.

By enabling students to showcase their real projects, we help companies recruit talent who are job-ready from day one.
